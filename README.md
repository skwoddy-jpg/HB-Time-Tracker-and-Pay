# HB-Time-Tracker-and-Pay

A simple and easy to use time tracking web app.
The app will auto save any data to your device keeping it personal to you.
Edit the hourly rate to reflect your pay rate.
Clock in at your planned start time (all times are editable after the period in case you forget.
Edit the pay period to the current month, using the first day after cutoff as your first date of the period.
Edit the pay period up to and including the cutoff day for next month.
The app will record all entries for that period.
You can save a period for future reference.


This app will evolve over time after any suggestions.
